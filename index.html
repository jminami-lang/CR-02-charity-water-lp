Our Story

Charity: Water is a nonprofit organization bringing clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries. We believe that everyone deserves access to clean water, and we work tirelessly to make that a reality.

Meet Amina

Amina used to walk miles every day to collect water for her family. Thanks to your support, her village now has a clean water well. Amina can go to school and her family is healthier and happier.
