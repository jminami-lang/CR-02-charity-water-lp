Our Story

Charity: Water is a nonprofit organization dedicated to bringing clean and safe drinking water to communities in developing countries. We believe that access to clean water is a basic human right, and we're committed to ending the water crisis through innovative, transparent, and community-led solutions. If you're a college student passionate about global justice and making a real impact, joining our mission means turning your energy and voice into life-changing action for people who need it most.

Meet Amina

Amina used to walk miles every day to collect water for her family. Thanks to your support, her village now has a clean water well. Amina can go to school and her family is healthier and happier.
